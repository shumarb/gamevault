Registration

Requirements

  • Name
    • The name must have at least 2 words.
    • The last word must have at least 2 letters.
    • The first word must start with an uppercase letter and must not begin with a space or any other character.
    • All other words must have least 3 letters.
    • Each word starts with an uppercase letter, followed by lowercase letters.

  • Username
    • The username must comprise at least 8 alphanumeric characters.

  • Email Address
    • Please use your GameVault email address.

  • Password
    • The password must have at least 3 uppercase letters.
    • The password must have at least 3 lowercase letters.
    • The password must have at least 2 numbers.
    • The password may contain 1 or more special characters.
